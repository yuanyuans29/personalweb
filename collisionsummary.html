Holiday Weekend Motor Vehicle Collisions Analysis

By Mengjia Sun

Introduction

New York City is a popular tourist desination,especially in summer. It's busy,chaotic streets full with pedestrians, cyclists, and cars. According to NYPD data, there're over 1.5 million of vehicle collisions occurred across all five nyc borough over the past 7 years. That is about 600 accidents per day.

The goal of this project is to explore the correlation among number of collision incidents and borough, street location, hours of day using varies d3.js and leaflet.js data visualization tools. It also identify the major cause of these collision incidents and the type of vehicle caused collision during 2019 Memorial Day weekend.

Project Scope

Location: all 5 boroughs in NYC

Timeframe: 2019 Memorial Day weekend, 5-25-2019 to 5-27-2019

Dataset Source

NYC OpenData - NYPD Motor Vehicle Collisions Crash

API Endpoint: https://data.cityofnewyork.us/resource/h9gi-nx95.json
